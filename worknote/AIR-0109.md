## Result
- `/image-gen` links to project-aware 2x2 crop.
- `/image-crop` accepts `project_id` and `start_scene`.
- Four panels map sequentially to project Scenes.
- Cropped files use `scene_NNN_crop.png`.
- Panels import directly into empty image slots and skip occupied/out-of-range Scenes.
- Crop imports use backend `replace_existing=false` protection to prevent race-time overwrite.
- Upscaled images and video clips reconnect by retaining the same Scene token.
- Upload order does not determine Scene order.
